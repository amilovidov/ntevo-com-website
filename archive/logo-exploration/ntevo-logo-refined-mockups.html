Refined Logo Concepts

Integrity Evolution

Exploring the rotating wheel motif through a Bauhaus lens, emphasizing mathematical precision and geometric relationships that reflect both integrity (wholeness, alignment) and evolution (transformation, progression).

Bauhaus Design Principles Applied:

  • Form follows function - Each shape positioned with mathematical precision
  • Primary colors + black - Yellow, blue, red with black accents
  • Geometric purity - Clean circles, squares, triangles without embellishment
  • Dynamic balance - Asymmetry creating visual tension and movement
  • Rationalist approach - Specific rotation angles (30°, 45°, 90°, 120°)

🔵 Integrity

Wholeness, completeness, core alignment, center point, coherence

🔄 Evolution

Transformation, rotation, progression, cycle, continuous improvement
